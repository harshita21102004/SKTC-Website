Return &Refund Policy

Read our return and refund policy to understand the conditions, eligibility, and refund process for your purchases.

1. Return Eligibility

Products can be returned within 7 days of delivery if they are damaged, defective, or different from the product ordered. The item must be unused and in its original packaging.

2. Non-Returnable Items

Customized products, digital items, gift cards, and products damaged due to customer misuse are not eligible for return.

3. Refund Process

Once your returned product is received and inspected, the refund will be processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

4. Exchange Policy

If you receive a defective or incorrect product, you may request a replacement instead of a refund, subject to product availability.

5. Return Shipping

Return shipping charges may be covered by us if the return is due to our error. Otherwise, the customer may be responsible for return shipping costs.

6. Return Request Process

To initiate a return, log in to your account, go to My Orders, select the product, and click Request Return. Our support team will review your request and contact you within 24–48 hours.

7. Cancellation Policy

Orders can be cancelled before they are shipped. Once the order has been dispatched, cancellation may not be possible and the Return Policy will apply.

8. Important Notes

Please keep the original invoice, packaging, accessories, and warranty card (if applicable) while requesting a return or replacement.

Shipping Policy

Learn about our shipping process, delivery timelines, and order handling procedures.

1. Order Processing

Orders are usually processed within 1–2 business days after successful payment confirmation. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays will be processed on the next working day.

2. Delivery Time

Estimated delivery time is between 3–7 business days depending on your location. Remote areas may require additional time.

3. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are calculated during checkout based on your delivery location and selected shipping method. Promotional offers may include free shipping on eligible orders.

4. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, a tracking ID will be shared via email or SMS so you can monitor your shipment in real time.

5. Delivery Delays

Delivery may be delayed due to weather conditions, natural disasters, logistics issues, or other unforeseen circumstances. We appreciate your patience in such cases.

6. Incorrect Shipping Address

Customers are responsible for providing a correct shipping address. We are not responsible for delays or failed deliveries caused by incorrect or incomplete address information.

7. Damaged or Lost Packages

If your package arrives damaged or is lost during transit, please contact our support team within 48 hours. We will investigate the issue with our delivery partner and provide a suitable resolution.

8. International Shipping

International shipping is available only for selected locations. Customs duties, taxes, and import charges (if any) are the responsibility of the customer.
